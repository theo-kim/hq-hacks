INNES

A drip from the fountain of knowledge

Innes is an experimental question answerer. You give us a question (in English as of right now) and we'll spit out an answer. No need for wierd phrasing or small words, our system will understand and proper English question (or give it its best shot) and give you an answer.

Currently, we are trying to teach our system to understand nuiassanced question phrasing and are doing so by analyzing games from the popular mobile trivia show, HQ. You can tune into our system every day at 3PM and 9PM EST to see how Innes stacks up.
